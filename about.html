About us.

Our design

Established in the year 1998 by Late Shri Shivji Lal and his two sons Shri Pramod Aggarwal and Shri Vinod Aggarwal, Day & Day Jeans ® is one of the prominent manufacturers of boundless range of men's wear. Our business is being carried forward by our third generation with best of the creativity and best of the modern approach of today's changing world.

Our focus

Equipped with a well established infrastructure and hi-tech Japanese technology, the company is proficient to provide quality range of products in bulk quantities. A special space is there to keep the products secured from unwanted damages.

Our design

Our design team brainstorms on a regular basis, to come up with new and trendy designs, which are created after extensive market research and regular feedback from our clients. They ensure that the designs are stylish, contemporary and wearable.

Our quality

Quality is our prime motto. The entire range of Mens Wear goes through rigorous quality testing during the various stages of manufacturing. We also test the finished apparels before dispatching these to clients to ensure that they get the best.
